Dr. Abdullah Al-Munifi

A surgeon specialized in bariatric surgery

Dr. Abdullah Al-Munaifi

Dr. Abdullah Al-Munaifi is considered one of the leading surgeons in the field of bariatric, advanced laparoscopic, and robotic surgery. Throughout his years of experience, he has combined precise scientific knowledge with extensive clinical practice to deliver advanced medical care based on the highest international standards. Dr. Abdullah is committed to ensuring that each patient’s health journey is clear and safe—from the initial evaluation, to selecting the most suitable procedure, and finally through continuous follow-up that guarantees the best long-term outcomes.

With more than 14 years of experience, Dr. Abdullah has worked in several major referral hospitals inside and outside the Kingdom, gaining diverse professional exposure in specialized international centers. This has enabled him to treat thousands of cases in bariatric and gastrointestinal surgery. He consistently applies the latest surgical and non-surgical techniques, tailoring each approach to the patient’s individual needs, with the goal of improving quality of life and supporting a sustainable healthy lifestyle.

Skills

Advanced Clinical Expertise in Bariatric and Laparoscopic Surgery

Throughout my years of work in bariatric and minimally invasive surgery, I have gained a wide range of surgical and non-surgical skills that I offer to my patients, always ensuring that I select the most suitable technique for each case to achieve the best possible outcomes.

  • Sleeve gastrectomy
  • Classic gastric bypass (Roux-en-Y)
  • Mini gastric bypass (OAGB)
  • Single anastomosis duodeno-ileal bypass (SADI)
  • Revisional bariatric surgery
  • Hiatal hernia and acid reflux surgery
  • Gallbladder removal
  • Appendectomy
  • Splenectomy
  • Stomach and intestinal tumor surgery
  • All types of hernia repairs
  • Diastasis recti repair
  • Abdominoplasty
  • Removal of lipomas and skin tumors
